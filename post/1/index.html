People have to go through at least one hike.

December 08, 2020 4197

“Have you ever thought about getting off here?”

“Numerous times.”

This is the most impressive impression of Wugong Mountain in autumn.

The idea of going hiking was a few years ago when I heard my friend talk about carrying dozens of kilograms of equipment, using aluminum cloth as a heating pad, and eating noodles in the cold snow. I have never had a chance to try.

Since the beginning of the school year, the studio decoration has attended classes and meetings in round of clinical treatment. Entering another stage of life, it is at a loss and hustle. More accurately, the restlessness generated after determining a node last year finally can’t help it. On the first weekend in the full sense, the booking of tickets began and restarted the travel plan completely disrupted by the epidemic. With the rhythm of life. Anyway, it seems that there is something that is slowly approaching what I have been looking for, although I don’t know what it is. But take your time. It can always be smoothed.
The journey was set to go out without brains at the beginning. The travel equipment was delayed on the road on Double Eleven, and the jacket was also sent from home urgently. It can be said that I received the goods three hours before departure.

I went to the convenience store temporarily to buy a few bags of dry food and two bottles of water, went back to the dormitory to pack and set out in the twilight. As a result, I failed to take more water, carry it to the top of the mountain and carry it back. The bags are getting lighter and lighter, and I just haven’t changed at all (even feel heavier in the end). My clothes were also saved at the foot of the mountain without thinking, took some personal clothes and went on foot without fear.

Oh, when I came back, I joked with my friends that this may be the most awkward, gray-faced and most unimagely travel I have experienced so far. It is not easy to see me like this: fancy slipping and wrestling, losing balance and rolling down the hillside, trembling to climb and down the mountain with stones. My autobiography has another unforgettable chapter. It’s the festival.

It’s hard to imagine that I climbed and rolled countless such slopes.
However, exhaustion can still give people a different experience, just like the happiness after working out and getting through the tired period. People have to go through at least one hike or rock climbing, whether it is willpower, control, endurance, or other things, it can bring more challenges and thinking to themselves.
I met a group of lovely friends again. Isn’t it true that memories are the feelings brought by the people accompanying you at that time. Communication with people in different majors or industries or ages can always bring different thoughts and inspirations.

From the moment I saw the flow of people after getting off the train, I was quite deeply touched when I was called back to reality. In the future, time and energy will only become less and less, but I still set aside a blank time a week for myself - whether it is dancing, playing the piano, cooking and reading, etc., it is always necessary to keep something and not lose the original idea. Thinking of going to practice the piano last week, I came back with a handful of ukulelele, and then another hobby can be included in the blank and idle time in a daze.
